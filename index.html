FAQ's

What is the Reservation Policy?

Walk–in guests will not be entertained. Deposit is required to confirm a reservation that is non refundable. The minimum deposit is 50% of the rental fee. A Security/Damage Deposit of P5,000.00 may be required. It is refundable if no damage nor violations. Additional when the amount of damage exceeds said deposit. Full remittance of the donation and security damage deposit must be remitted 3 weeks before the scheduled date. If the full donation is not given on the due date, the reserved date is automatically canceled, and the reservation deposit is forfeited. Check-in time is 3:00 PM and Check-Out Time is 12:00 NN.

How to cancel Reservation?

Cancellations made 15 or more days before check-in date will incur a cancellation charge of 20%. Cancellations made more than 7 days in advance but less than 15 days will incur a cancellation charge of 40%. Cancellation made 48 hours before the scheduled event date will incur a cancellation charge of 50%. In case the guest fails to appear in the Center by 6 p.m. on the reserved date, the fee for the room shall be charged to him/her. Any refund will take at least 3 weeks to process.

What is the Check-in Policy?

Check-in @ 3 pm and Check-out @ 12nn two hours before 12nn the guest will be reminded Any extension shall be communicated to the office at least 3 hours before the scheduled check out. A fee of P 250.00/ hour will be charged for the extension. A minimum of 1-hour extension is allowed Electrical appliances (rice cooker, stove, (karaoke) are prohibited to be brought in.

What is the Postponement Policy?

Postponement or rescheduling of the visit without penalty can only be made 7 days before the original date otherwise a penalty of 30% of the transaction cost will be imposed.
