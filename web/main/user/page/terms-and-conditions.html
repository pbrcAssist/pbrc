If the full donation is not given on the due date, the reserved date is automatically canceled, and the reservation deposit is forfeited. Check-in time is 3:00 PM and Check-Out Time is 12:00 NN.

How to cancel Reservation?

Cancellations made 15 or more days before check-in date will incur a cancellation charge of 20%. Cancellations made more than 7 days in advance but less than 15 days will incur a cancellation charge of 40%. Cancellation made 48 hours before the scheduled event date will incur a cancellation charge of 50%. In case the guest fails to appear in the Center by 6 p.m. on the reserved date, the fee for the room shall be charged to him/her. Any refund will take at least 3 weeks to process.

Reservation (Booking) Procedures

By proceeding with the reservation, you agree to adhere to the following terms and conditions:

Stage 1: Receiving and Registration

Receive the guest in front of the house

  • Upon arrival, our team will open the door and extend a warm welcome to you.
  • The doorman will coordinate with the bell desk to ensure the prompt pickup of your baggage.
  • In the event that the bell desk team is unavailable, the doorman will personally assist with your baggage.
  • Your journey to the reception desk will be accompanied by an escort, typically the bellboy or porter.
  • As a gesture of hospitality, you will be offered a welcome drink and, if applicable, a welcome gift like a Spice Garland, Spice box, Juice, etc.

Receive the guests at the reception

  • We aim to promptly welcome you with a cheerful and pleasant smile.
  • Addressing you by the appropriate surname, if known, is part of our personalized service.
  • For reservation verification, we kindly request details such as your last name, confirmation number, and booking source.
  • To complete your check-in, we require presentation of your ID card or passport.
  • If applicable, please provide your business card for our records.

Register the guest - Complete the registration card or register the guest

  • Ensuring the accuracy of your information, we will verify details against your reservation, including your name, date, and payment mechanism.
  • Our team will stamp the check-in time on the registration card for your reference.
  • Check-in details will be entered into our hotel software/PMS system.
  • Before finalizing, we will review the registration card for your signature.
  • Room preferences will be discussed based on availability, or an available "ready to let” room will be allocated to you.
  • We will note your expected departure details, as well as any special requests, airport drop-offs, etc.

Stage 2: Allocation of the Room

Continuing with the reservation procedures, the following terms and conditions apply to the room allocation stage:

  • Room allocation involves assigning a vacant and ready (VR) room to you for your stay.
  • For returning guests, rooms will be assigned based on your specified preferences.
  • Accurate room status information is maintained through a room status board or room rack at the reception.
  • Upon room allocation, the reception will:
    • Block or code the room as sold for your exclusive use.
    • Prepare a key card for the assigned room, including details such as arrival and departure dates, rate, and offer it to you for your signature.
    • Hand over the room key either directly to you or to the bellboy for your convenience.
    • Record your arrival on the daily arrival list.

Stage 3: Secure Advance Payment

To ensure the security of your reservation, advance payment is required. Please note the following terms and conditions:

  • Advance payment can be made through a cash deposit or credit card authorization.
  • When handling cash or cheque deposits:
    • A receipt will be issued, and the payment will be recorded in your ledger.
  • For credit card payments:
    • We will evaluate the credit limit and availability of the card.
    • An imprint of the card will be taken, and your signature will be obtained.
    • A pre-authorization (pre-auth) will be obtained from the Credit Card company or EDC machine for the required amount, and the details will be recorded in the reservation.

Stage 4: Information Service

As part of our commitment to providing exceptional service, the following terms and conditions govern the information service stage:

  • Our receptionist plays a crucial role in informing guests about the extensive facilities and services offered by our hotel.
  • This information is designed to help guests familiarize themselves with our hotel amenities and contribute to increased sales.
  • At this stage, our receptionist will:
    • Acknowledge guests about the hotel's activities, facilities, and services, providing clear directions and information about our location.
    • Enquire about the need for a wake-up call. If required, this will be recorded accurately to ensure a seamless stay experience.

Stage 5: Complete the Check-In Formalities

Upon completion of the check-in formalities, the following terms and conditions guide our procedures:

  • The bellboy or Guest Relations Executive (GRE) is designated to escort guests up to their allocated room, ensuring a smooth transition.
  • We will promptly inform other departments and sections about your arrival through an arrival notification slip.

Stage 6: Open the Guest Folder

Efficient management of your stay involves the following terms and conditions in opening the guest folder:

  • We will open your guest bill/folder, ensuring any old bill copies are cleared for transparency and accuracy.
  • Key information for billing purposes, including room rate, number of nights, departure date, and mode of payment, will be meticulously recorded.
  • The guest bill will be filed in your designated folder box, along with the completed registration card, for comprehensive record-keeping.

Departure (Check Out) Procedures

By proceeding with the checkout process, you agree to adhere to the following terms and conditions:

The objective of the checkout process is to efficiently settle the guest’s account, ensuring accuracy to prevent erroneous charges for the guest and financial losses for the lodging establishment.

The guest checkout involves the following steps:

  1. Guest requests checkout.
  2. Desk clerk inquires about the quality of products and services.
  3. Guest returns the key to the desk clerk.
  4. Desk clerk retrieves a hard copy of the electronic folder.
  5. Desk clerk reviews the folder for completeness.
  6. Guest reviews charges and payments.
  7. Guest determines the method of payment.
  8. Guest makes payment.
  9. Desk clerk files the folder and related documents for the audit.
  10. Desk clerk communicates guest departure to housekeeping and other departments in the center if necessary.

Setting our objectives is paramount, focusing on accommodating guests and maintaining necessary data to provide the center with information for the income statement. The outlined steps for guest checkout demonstrate the ease of establishing operational procedures when keeping these goals in mind.

By proceeding, you acknowledge and accept these terms and conditions governing the Departure (Check Out) Procedures.
